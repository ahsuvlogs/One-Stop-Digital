About ABHA Card

The ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) card is a unique health ID that stores all your medical records on a digital platform. With the help of this card, you can easily share your health-related information with doctors, hospitals, and healthcare providers.

Benefits of ABHA Card

  • Free and lifetime valid digital health ID.
  • Secure storage of all your medical history.
  • Easy access to health records anywhere in India.
  • Supports Ayushman Bharat scheme benefits.
  • Fully linked with your Aadhaar details.
