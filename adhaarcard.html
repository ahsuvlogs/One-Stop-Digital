About Aadhaar Card

The Aadhaar Card is a unique identification document issued by the Government of India to residents. It contains a 12-digit unique identification number along with demographic and biometric information, enabling easy verification of identity across the country.

Benefits of Aadhaar Card

  • Serves as proof of identity and address anywhere in India.
  • Required for accessing various government subsidies and services.
  • Used for opening bank accounts, getting a SIM card, and more.
  • Facilitates easy verification for online and offline services.
  • Linked with various welfare schemes for direct benefit transfers.
