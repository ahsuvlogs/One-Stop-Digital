About Driving License

A Driving License is an official document issued by the government that authorizes an individual to operate one or more types of motor vehicles. It is a mandatory requirement for legally driving on public roads.

Benefits of a Driving License

  • Legal authorization to drive vehicles.
  • Serves as a valid identity proof.
  • Required for vehicle insurance claims.
  • Helps in vehicle registration processes.
  • Accepted as an ID in various official processes.
