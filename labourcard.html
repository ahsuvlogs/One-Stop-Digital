About Labour Card

The Labour Card is an identification document issued to registered workers. Through this card, workers can access welfare schemes, health benefits, and insurance coverage.

Benefits of Labour Card

  • Access to Government welfare schemes.
  • Accident insurance coverage.
  • Educational benefits for workers' children.
  • Health and medical assistance.
  • Old age pension and other benefits.
