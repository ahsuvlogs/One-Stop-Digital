About Masked Aadhaar Card

The Masked Aadhaar Card is a secure version of the Aadhaar card where the first 8 digits of the Aadhaar number are hidden and only the last 4 digits are visible. This format is recommended for situations where you need to prove your identity without revealing your full Aadhaar number.

Benefits of Masked Aadhaar Card

  • Enhanced privacy and security by hiding most digits of your Aadhaar number.
  • Accepted as valid proof of identity for most services.
  • Can be downloaded online from the UIDAI website.
  • Useful for hotel bookings, SIM card purchases, and other KYC processes.
  • Prevents misuse of your Aadhaar details.
