About MGNREGA Job Card

The MGNREGA Job Card is issued under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005, to rural households in India. It enables eligible individuals to apply for work and ensures transparency in the implementation of employment schemes.

Key Features

  • Proof of registration under MGNREGA scheme.
  • Contains worker’s details, photograph, and unique job card number.
  • Ensures right to 100 days of wage employment in a year.
  • Helps in tracking work and wage payments.
  • Issued free of cost by Gram Panchayats.
