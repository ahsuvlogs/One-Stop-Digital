About PAN Card

A Permanent Account Number (PAN) is a unique, ten-character alphanumeric identifier issued by the Indian Income Tax Department. It is essential for financial transactions such as opening a bank account, receiving taxable salary, or selling property above specified limits.

Benefits of PAN Card

  • Serves as valid identity proof.
  • Mandatory for filing income tax returns.
  • Helps track financial transactions and prevent tax evasion.
  • Can be applied online or offline easily.
  • Ensures quick and hassle-free processing with our assistance.
