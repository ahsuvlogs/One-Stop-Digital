About Ration Card

A Ration Card is an official document issued by the government that enables eligible households to purchase essential food grains and supplies at subsidized rates through the Public Distribution System (PDS).

Benefits of a Ration Card

  • Provides subsidized food grains and essential commodities.
  • Acts as proof of identity and residence.
  • Required for certain government welfare schemes.
  • Helps ensure food security for low-income families.
  • Accepted as a supporting document for various applications.
