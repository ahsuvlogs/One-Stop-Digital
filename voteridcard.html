About Voter ID Card

A Voter ID Card is an identity document issued by the Election Commission of India to eligible citizens of India, enabling them to cast their vote in elections. It also serves as an official proof of identity and address.

Benefits of a Voter ID Card

  • Grants the right to vote in elections.
  • Serves as a valid identity and address proof.
  • Required for government schemes and subsidies.
  • Helps in ensuring fair democratic participation.
  • Accepted in various official and financial processes.
